About this soundtrack

Music is everywhere in Against the Loveless World — on radios in salons being painted, in a courtroom turned defiant stage, in the oud Mhammad tunes on a quiet night, in the dabke Nahr leads at a friend's wedding.

Some tracks are named in the book. Others are best-guess representatives of a scene's era and mood. The full playlist is hosted on YouTube.

The novel

Against the Loveless World by Susan Abulhawa — published 2020 by Atria Books / Bloomsbury. Winner of the Palestine Book Award.

Nahr, a Palestinian woman, recounts her life from a soundproofed Israeli prison cell — from Kuwait to Amman to occupied Palestine.
